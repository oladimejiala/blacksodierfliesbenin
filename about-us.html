About AMTECH-Farms Benin

Pioneering sustainable agriculture through Black Soldier Fly technology

Our Story

Founded in 2020, AMTECH-Farms Benin began as a small-scale initiative to address the growing need for sustainable fruits sources in West Africa's livestock industry. Now, what started as an experimental BSF farm has grown into Benin's leading producer of high-quality Black Soldier Fly larvae and related agricultural products.

Today, we operate a state-of-the-art insect farming facility that converts organic waste into valuable animal feed, helping farmers reduce costs while promoting environmental sustainability.

Why Black Soldier Flies?

Black Soldier Fly larvae (BSFL) represent the future of sustainable animal nutrition:

  • High Protein Content: 40-60% protein, ideal for poultry, fish and livestock
  • Eco-Friendly: Reduces organic waste by up to 70% in just 15 days
  • Cost Effective: Up to 30% cheaper than conventional feed options
  • Natural: No antibiotics or growth hormones

Our Mission

To revolutionize West Africa's agricultural sector through innovative insect-based solutions that promote food security, environmental sustainability, and economic empowerment for local farmers.

Our Vision

To become the leading provider of sustainable animal nutrition solutions in West Africa by 2030, reducing reliance on imported feed while creating a circular economy around organic waste.
