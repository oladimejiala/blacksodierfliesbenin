Black Soldier Fly (BSF) Larvae: A Sustainable Protein Revolution

What Are BSF Larvae?

Black Soldier Fly larvae (BSFL) are nutrient-rich, eco-friendly, and widely regarded as one of the best alternative protein sources for livestock, poultry, and aquaculture. They help reduce organic waste while producing high-quality feed.

Protein Content: ~45-55%
Fat Content: ~30%
Rich in: Calcium, amino acids, and antimicrobial peptides.
