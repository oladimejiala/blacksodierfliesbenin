Live vs. Dried Larvae

Live Larvae (560 CFA/kg):

  • Ideal for immediate use
  • Higher palatability for poultry
  • Contains natural enzymes that aid digestion

Dried Larvae (1,100 CFA/kg):

  • 6-12 month shelf life
  • Easier to mix with other feed
  • Higher protein concentration (removes moisture)
